University of Alberta Biomedical Technologies Development Group

An undergraduate student group for those interested in medicine, engineering, and science.

Established in Fall 2018, UAlberta Biomed is a student group for innovative and hardworking students who are interested in creating solutions in the medicine, engineering, and science fields of study. Whether you're an engineering student, a science student, or a business student, UAlberta Biomed is the club for you. Our group encourages new and creative ideas to be explored.

Our Plan

The Competition

DEBUT is a US based competition, strictly for undergraduate students. The competition requires teams to identify a clinical problem and build a fully functional prototype to solve it. The deadline for submission is May 2019.
For more information, visit https://venturewell.org/debut/

The Project

The project of the year will be an exoskeleton for the workplace. The purpose of the exoskeleton will be to correct posture and reduce the load for the back and shoulders. See the 'Project Proposal' under the 'Club Documents' tab for more information.

The Team

Joining our group will not only provide you with invaluable research experience. You will also gain great team building experience. On top of this, you will have the opportunity to meet unique and inspiring individuals.
